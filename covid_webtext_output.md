## Hospital Admissions

* The CovidHub ensemble's one-week-ahead forecast predicts that new weekly laboratory-confirmed COVID-19 hospital admissions will be approximately 10300 nationally, with 6400 to 14300 likely reported in the week ending January 17, 2026.
